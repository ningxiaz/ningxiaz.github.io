FBLESSSpend less time on FB and you'll be blessed, otherwise...

FBless is a Chrome plugin that tracks your time spent on Facebook everyday, and auto-posts a message of shame on your timeline once you go beyond the limit you sets for yourself. Once installed, it starts tracking in the background. Today's performance can be viewed in the popup window and everyday statistics can be viewed on the "options" page, where you can also adjust your daily goal/limit if you want.

FBless was built for a research project I did for CS376 Research Topics in HCI. The research question is to investigate whether applications that automatically broadcast messages of failures will help reach daily goals towards behavior change and habit formation. After a between-subject study using FBless, it's concluded that more considerations, such as internal self-motivation, privacy concerns, are needed for designing applications with similar features.

More information about the research project can be in this slide.

It's built with Chrome API, Node.js and Facebook Node SDK.
