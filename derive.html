DÉRIVEDerive more meanings for your travels.

Dérive is an interactive virtual guestbook targeting at travellers in spaces such as airports and hotels. With Leap Motion technologies, Travellers can sign their names in air, indicate their origins and destinations, then a unique star will be generated based on features of their signatures in the virtual universe together with other travellers' stars. They can explore the universe to see other people's comments poped up on each star and contribute their own tips or feelings by scanning the QR code and inputting on their smartphones. A globe gesture will transition the universe view to a globe view where travelers can see where their fellow travelers come from and where they are going to.

Dérive is made with the help of Leap.js, WebGL, Three.js, Express, MongoDB and Socket.io. A live demo can be found here.
