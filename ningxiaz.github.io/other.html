OTHER DESIGNS

This t-shirt design was for the celebration of the 10th anniversary of UCD Fudan Joint Degree program. The new students of the 2010 class wore the t-shirts to attend the welcome ceremony.

When I thought about the number ten, I found that the symbol of Ireland -- shamrock actually looks like the character ten in Chinese (a cross means 10 in Chinese). Meanwhile, one type of Chinese knot also resembles this character. So I decided to put shamrock and Chinese lucky knot on each side of the t-shirt. In order to convey the idea that our cultures interleave in the joint program, I put Chinese characters with shamrock and English words with Chinese knot. The Chinese character "nian" (meaning years) was written by me with a online calligraphy tool. At last, I put the logos of both universities on each side in the corners so that when you actually wear the t-shirt, they are together.

In 2010 I participated an online design contest of Ray-Ban glasses on douban.com and designed this imprint pattern for Ray-Ban's Wayfarer. Finally I won the Most Favorite Design Prize. I love music and pop culture. So I decided to merge the logo of Radiohead with a bit of Andy Warhol. So there it was. Very simple idea but people really liked it.
