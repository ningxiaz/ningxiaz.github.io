INSPIXELEvery Day with Color!

Inspixel is an iPhone web app that organizes inspirational images by color for visual designers or anyone interested in colors. When you take a picture with it or import one from the albums on your phone, Inspixel helps to extract a beautiful color palette from the picture, which can be applied to design projects. You can easily attach tags and Geolocations to the picture. When the pictures start to pile up, you won't get lost in all your inspirations as Inspixel organizes them by color categories and tags. Besides, it also allows favoriting for quick access and sharing to Facebook.

The live version is here! We've also made a promotional video about it.

It is the final project for CS147 Introduction to HCI at Stanford, completed by me and two other Stanford students using jQuery Mobile framework, PHP and MySql. It has received positive feedbacks from the teaching staff of CS147 as well as the Cofounder of Instagram.

We have tried several other ideas about inspiration before coming to this one, which focuses on a specific group of users. We've also gone through several cycles of prototyping, heuristic evaluation, user testing, making changes and debugging. I have learned not only incredibly useful tools and techniques, but also valuable communication and teamwork skills.

I have participated in all the design, developing and testing phrases and am responsible for majority of the code, including front-end and back-end.
